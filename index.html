Overview

Mission Statement

This journal club aims to provide a forum for graph-ML, deep generative models and computational neuroscience with diverse machine learning and neuroscience backgrounds. Weekly meetings will focus on a paper relevant to a theoretical understanding of some graph neural network problem or system. Graduate students are especially encouraged to attend and propose/present papers. Appropriate papers present data, theoretical models, foundational principles for computation, new statistical, mathematical, or machine learning methods, etc.
